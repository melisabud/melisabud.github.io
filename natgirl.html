"'naturally' a girl" prelinger archive edit


"'naturally' a girl," video on being transfem

statement: although i am not transgender, i think about my fellow lgbt+ friends who are. as a disabled lesbian, i can relate to the struggles of being in the minority. what if a video compiled of clips from the very binary-focused late 1900s tried desperately to express a different perspective...?

notes: this is a school project. i am in no way claiming any of these clips as my own. these clips are simply compiled in a montage to create a new meaning!
