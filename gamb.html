the arduino board nostalgia gamble! the loser gets rickrolled

statement: i wanted to create a fun little device that has a game-like feature to it when people interact with it. with a randomizer coded in, the person who presses the button doesn't know what they'll get.

notes: there are five results a person can get: rickroll, piano cat, oo ee oo ah ah ting tang walla walla bing bang, caramelldansen, and nyan cat. nyan cat being my favorite! the rickroll will light up a red LED, indicating a "game over." blast throught the past with this lil fun gadget!
